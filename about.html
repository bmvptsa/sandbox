About BMV PTSA

Building strong partnerships between families, educators, and community to support every child's success

Our Mission & Vision

As part of the National and Florida PTA network, we are committed to making every child's potential a reality by engaging and empowering families and communities to advocate for all children.

Our Mission

To make every child's potential a reality by engaging and empowering families and communities to advocate for all children at Beachside Montessori Village.

Our Vision

To be the premier advocacy association for children and families, creating a thriving educational environment where every student succeeds.

Our Core Values

As part of the National and Florida PTA network, we uphold core values that guide our work and decision-making:

Collaboration

Working in partnership with diverse stakeholders to broaden our impact

Commitment

Dedicated to children's educational success, health, and well-being

Diversity

Acknowledging the potential of everyone without regard to background

Respect

Valuing individual contributions as we work toward our goals

Accountability

Shared responsibility for achieving our association's initiatives

What We Do

The BMV PTSA serves our school community through various initiatives and programs that directly benefit students, families, and teachers:

Advocacy

Supporting policies that benefit students and families at local, state, and national levels through the PTA network

Educational Programs

Hosting workshops, speaker events, and educational opportunities for families and students

Community Building

Creating connections between families, teachers, and school administration through events and activities

Student Support

Funding programs, resources, and supplies that directly benefit student learning and well-being

Volunteer Coordination

Organizing volunteer opportunities for school events, classroom support, and special activities

Ethical Fundraising

Supporting school programs through responsible fundraising activities that follow PTA guidelines
